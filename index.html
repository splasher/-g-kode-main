📖 How G-KODE Works

For Clients:
1. Register with your details, photo, and ID.
2. Post a gig and optionally share your location.
3. A worker accepts — chat opens immediately.
4. Worker navigates to you using GPS directions.
5. Work is done. Rate each other.

For Workers:
1. Register with your profession, photo, and ID.
2. Browse open gigs near you.
3. Accept a gig — chat with the client instantly.
4. Tap "Navigate to Client" for GPS directions.
5. Do the work. Build your reputation.

For Businesses:
1. Register your business.
2. Add products with prices.
3. Sell on the Marketplace.
4. 3% G-KODE fee auto-deducted.

Emergency:
Tap 🚨 anytime. Police, Fire, Ambulance on speed dial.
