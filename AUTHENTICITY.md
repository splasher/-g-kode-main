# AUTHENTICITY & TRUST

## G-KODE — Kenya Helping Kenya 🇰🇪

## Our Commitment to Authenticity

G-KODE is built on the foundation of **trust** and **authenticity**. Every user on our platform is verified through:

1. **Phone Number Verification** — Each user must have a valid Kenyan phone number
2. **National ID Verification** — Users upload their ID for verification
3. **Profile Photo Verification** — Real photos, not AI-generated
4. **Profession Verification** — Skills are validated through the community

## Reporting Concerns

If you encounter any suspicious activity, report it immediately:
1. Go to the Complaint section
2. Select the reason
3. Provide details
4. Our admin will review within 24 hours

## Data Protection

We comply with the **Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019**:
- Your data is secure and encrypted
- We never share your personal information
- You can request your data anytime
- You can delete your data anytime
