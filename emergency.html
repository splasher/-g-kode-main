💡 Emergency Tips

  • Stay Calm — Panic makes everything harder
  • Call 999 — Works from all networks in Kenya
  • Share Location — Use the button above to send your GPS coordinates
  • Know Your Location — Be ready to describe where you are
  • Follow Instructions — Listen to the emergency operator
  • Don't Hang Up — Stay on the line until they tell you to
