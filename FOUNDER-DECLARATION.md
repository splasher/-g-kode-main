# G-KODE — FOUNDER'S DECLARATION

## To Whom It May Concern

I, **Splasher**, am the sole Founder and Creator of **G-KODE**.

## My Story

G-KODE was born from my personal experience walking the tarmac in Kenya, searching for work with no success. I saw plumbers sitting idle, hairdressers with empty chairs, graduates with degrees but no jobs, and families with problems they couldn't fix because they didn't know who to call.

I asked myself: "What if every Kenyan's skill could be seen? What if the solution was already in our neighborhoods?"

G-KODE is the answer.

## My Commitment

1. G-KODE will never charge hidden fees or monthly subscriptions.
2. G-KODE will remain Kenyan — built for our people, our culture, our needs.
3. G-KODE will protect users through transparency, escrow, and accountability.
4. G-KODE will always put the community first — not investors, not advertisers.

## My Vision

A Kenya where no skill is wasted, no crisis goes unanswered, and every person can earn with dignity.

## My Promise

If G-KODE ever breaks these commitments, every Kenyan has the right to hold me accountable.
